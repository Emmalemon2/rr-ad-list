Everything is sorted by it's page based on the destination url in reverse alphbetical order (primarily affected by the fiction number, not the title). Thirst trap purgatory

All the ads whos main selling point is just an oversexualised image, and a few characters that were just unnecessarily well endowed. Also a couple that looked at me funny while I was sorting. I let a couple of harem story ads pass, because they're not really "traps," they're just plain thirsty.

Meme ads

Probably why any readers are here. Not much to say.

Single aspect ads

These are the ads that pick out one aspect of a story and promote that. It has all the ads of some guy staring at a big monster. Look here for any ads that emphasize one moment, decision, or theme, while leaving the rest of the story with much less detail.

Cover ads

Anything I could envision on either side of a paperback cover. Ranges from a picture with cover vibes, to literal book covers. This is where to find ads that cram in all the info about what to expect from a story.

Other ads

Exists to spare me hard decisions. Contains a bunch of ads that could have gone in any catagory, a few unfunny (according to me) memes, and the only picture of a real person in any of these ads.
